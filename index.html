Basic Info About Me

I am a passionate Android App Developer with expertise in Java and Kotlin, dedicated to building user-centric applications that seamlessly combine intuitive design with robust functionality. My skills include UI/UX design, localization, remote configuration, ads integration, and app performance optimization. Leveraging tools like Crashlytics and ANR monitoring, I ensure app stability and high-quality performance.

I have successfully implemented ads using Remote Config and Firestore, optimizing ad show rates to enhance app monetization. My proficiency with the MVVM architecture, Firebase, and SQLite enables me to deliver innovative solutions that drive user engagement. I am committed to continuous learning and creating impactful applications that exceed user expectations.

In my recent role at MediaDecode, I led the development of applications like Image to Text | OCR Scanner, an advanced OCR tool that efficiently converts images into editable text, Screen Translator, which integrates real-time text recognition and translation from mobile screen, and Text On Photo, a feature-rich text editor app. These projects highlight my ability to deliver complex functionalities while maintaining a user-friendly interface. I have also contributed to many more innovative applications, showcasing my versatility and commitment to excellence in Android development.
